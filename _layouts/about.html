Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services.

What is Crypto Airdrop Archive?

Crypto Airdrop Archive is a comprehensive collection of cryptocurrency projects and airdrops, organized chronologically for easy browsing and research. We provide detailed information about each project, including eligibility criteria, distribution details, and historical context.

How do I participate in an airdrop?

Each project has its own requirements for participation. Click on a project to see detailed instructions, eligibility criteria, and participation steps. We regularly update our listings to ensure you have the most current information.

How is the data verified?

We cross-reference information from multiple reliable sources, including official project documentation, community reports, and on-chain data. Our team verifies each listing to ensure accuracy and completeness of information.

Can I suggest a project or correction?

Yes! We welcome community contributions. You can suggest new projects or corrections through our contact form, email, or GitHub repository. All suggestions are reviewed by our team to maintain the quality of our archive.
