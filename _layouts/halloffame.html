Methodology

Projects in the Hall of Fame were selected and ranked based on the following criteria:

Historical Impact

How the airdrop influenced subsequent token distribution models and set precedents in the industry.

Total Value

The approximate total value of tokens distributed at the time of the airdrop.

Distribution Fairness

How equitably tokens were distributed among participants.

Innovation

Novel approaches to eligibility criteria, distribution mechanisms, or governance implementation.

Long-term Success

The continued relevance and value of the project after the airdrop.

This ranking is periodically updated to reflect new information and changing perspectives on historical significance.
